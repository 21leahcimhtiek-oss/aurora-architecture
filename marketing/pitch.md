# Aurora Rayes Architecture Positioning

## One-Liner

Aurora Rayes Architecture documents the operating system behind the Aurora Rayes product ecosystem.

## Short Pitch

This repository explains how Aurora Rayes coordinates agents, repositories, deployments, marketing workflows, and diagnostics. It is intended for internal enablement and partner confidence, not direct monetization.

## Key Messages

- Clear orchestration model for multi-repository operations
- Safety-first handling of secrets, payments, and release workflows
- Practical documentation for implementation teams and partner reviews
